Evaluating Human Perception On Cateogrical Scatterplots

The purpose of this research study is to understand how people interpret visualized information. You are being asked to take part in a research study because you are between 18 and 65 years of age.

Being in a research study is completely voluntary. You can choose not to be in this research study. You can also say yes now and change your mind later.

If you agree to take part in this research, you will be shown a series of visualizations and asked to use those visualizations to estimate specific statistics or patterns. Your participation in this study will take less than 20 minutes. You can choose to stop taking the survey at any time. You must be at least 18 years old to participate. If you are younger than 18 years old, please do not proceed with this study.
